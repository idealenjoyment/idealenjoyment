### Projects

**[Algo Trading & Backtesting Suite](https://github.com/idealenjoyment/stocks)**
Live market data over Binance WebSocket, order simulation, and a Python backtesting layer. Built to actually test trading ideas rather than just think about them.

**[GDWA -- Gold Denominated World Assets](https://github.com/idealenjoyment/GDWA)**
A dashboard that reprices global assets in gold rather than USD. The premise: the dollar is a moving ruler, gold is a fixed one. What does the S&P actually look like if you price it in something that can't be printed?

**[VALM -- Vedic Astrology Life Map](https://github.com/idealenjoyment/VALM)**
A full-stack birth chart engine built on Swiss Ephemeris (`pyswisseph`) for precise sidereal calculations. Renders South Indian, North Indian, and ecliptic wheel charts in SVG, runs a rule-based interpretation engine covering yogas, nakshatras, Rahu/Ketu axis and more, and has an interactive transit timeline you can scrub forward and backward in time. Flask backend, zero frontend frameworks. One of those projects that went much deeper than originally planned.
